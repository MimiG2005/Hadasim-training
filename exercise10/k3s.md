# K3s

## 1. What is it?
K3s is a lightweight and modular version of Kubernetes, designed to simplify the installation and management of Kubernetes clusters, especially in resource-constrained environments.

## 2. When should it be used?
K3s is suitable when you want to run Kubernetes on small machines or with limited resources, such as personal computers, small servers, or edge devices.

## 3. Where can it be used?
- On-premises systems
- Personal computers for development and testing
- Small servers or edge devices (IoT, edge)
- Private or public clouds, especially if a lightweight and fast solution is desired

## 4. Why should I use it?
- Simple and quick installation
- Low resource consumption
- Easier maintenance and management compared to full Kubernetes
- Suitable for development, testing, and small to medium-sized applications
